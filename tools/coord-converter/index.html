OSGB36 to ETRS89 Converter

This tool provides high-precision, bidirectional coordinate transformations between three major UK survey grids:

  • OSGB36: The familiar Ordnance Survey National Grid.
  • ETRS89: The standard GPS-compatible system for Europe (effectively WGS84).
  • LSG: The London Survey Grid, used for major infrastructure projects.

The transformations are based on the official OSTN15 model from Ordnance Survey and the 7-parameter Helmert transformation specified by Transport for London for the LSG. This ensures the highest possible accuracy for engineering and survey applications.

This tool is still under development. Use with caution and check all results before use.
